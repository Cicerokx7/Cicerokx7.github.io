This early fascination has guided me through a series of dynamic projects and stimulating coding competitions, often alongside the Dallas Personal Robotics Group (DPRG).

As my academic path unfolded, I continued to expand my coding horizons, undertaking an array of school projects that served as catalysts for my software development skills. These academic pursuits were not only enlightening but also equipped me to practically apply computer science concepts to a multitude of challenges.

This experience exposed me to the immersive world of virtual environments, cementing my role as a versatile software engineer capable of developing interactive and engaging applications.

My commitment to problem-solving and my love for technology have propelled me into the realm of computer science, ready to take on fresh challenges and explore innovative solutions.
