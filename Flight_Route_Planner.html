Flight Route Planner

The Project

I developed an innovative Airport Routing Optimization Software as part of a college assignment, aiming to provide efficient travel solutions for passengers. This software was designed to take a list of airports and their connections, considering both time and cost constraints. The user specifies a starting and final destination, and the software utilizes an iterative backtracking algorithm to find the top three fastest routes and the top three least expensive routes, offering travelers a variety of options tailored to their preferences.

What I Learned

This project demonstrated my ability to tackle complex problems by implementing a robust algorithm that effectively manages airport connections, time, and cost considerations. It showcased my proficiency in software development, data manipulation, and algorithm design. This experience has further refined my problem-solving skills and has been an invaluable addition to my portfolio, highlighting my dedication to creating practical solutions that enhance user experiences and optimize decision-making in real-world scenarios.
